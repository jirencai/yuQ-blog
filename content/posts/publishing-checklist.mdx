# A Lightweight Publishing Checklist

Shipping quickly is good, but publishing without checks creates avoidable rework.

## Before every post

1. Confirm title and summary match the real topic.
2. Confirm date and tag values are correct.
3. Read once on mobile width and once on desktop width.

![Release checklist board](/images/cover-checklist.svg "Lightweight checks before every publish")

## Before every release

- Verify canonical URL and sitemap generation.
- Check social metadata preview.
- Remove stale draft notes and placeholder text.

> A short checklist prevents most last-minute publishing mistakes.
