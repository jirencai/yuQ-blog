# Welcome to yuQ Blog

This blog publishes practical notes about writing, delivery workflow, and small-project maintainability.

## What to expect

- Short posts with clear structure.
- Production-oriented checklists and conventions.
- Content-first updates with minimal process overhead.

![Writing desk scene](/images/cover-writing-flow.svg "The default visual style used for short notes")

## Publishing rule

Each post should be understandable on its own and should help the reader ship something useful.

> Clear writing scales better than complicated process.
